Who I Am

I grew up in a small town on a dead end road. I have been playing with design since my parents brought home our first computer when I was 11. Since then I've clocked many hours in front of a computer screen, tinkering and creating.

I’ve always tried to foster my creative side—over the years delving into music, writing, film-making and graphics. To give some perspective, my very first website was a page filled with pictures of my favorite Pokémon.

Since then, I have worked hard on finding ways to create a great balance between creativity and technique, so that I might inspire passion and creativity in others with my work.

“What is design? It's where you stand with a foot in two worlds - the world of technology and the world of people and human purposes - and you try to bring the two together.”
– Mitchell Kapor Projects
