Who I Am

I grew up in a small town in Michigan, and I have been playing with design since I was very young. My parents brought home our first computer when I was 11, and since then I've clocked many hours in front of a computer screen.

I’ve always tried to foster my creative side—trying my hand over the years at music, writing, filmmaking and graphics. To give some perspective, my very first website was a page filled with pictures of my favorite Pokémon.

Since then, I have worked hard on finding ways to create a great balance between creativity and technique, so that I might inspire passion and creativity in others with my work.

“Curiosity about life in all of its aspects, I think, is still the secret of great creative people”
– Leo Burnett Projects
